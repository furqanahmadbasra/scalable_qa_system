[Query 2] What happens if a student fails a course?
  [LLM Answer] According to Excerpt 1, if a student fails a course, they will receive an F grade. Additionally, if a student fails a Major Field Course, they will not be promoted to the next semester (7th semester for Bachelor of Industrial Design and 9th semester for Bachelor of Architecture) as per Excerpt 2, point c.

If a student fails a Minor subject, they will not be reflected in the student's transcript, but they will have to pass all four courses to be eligible for the Minor. If a student does not clear four courses, then the courses with pass grades will be reflected in the transcripts as Additional Courses (Excerpt 3, point 15).

[Query 8] What are the requirements for graduation?
  [LLM Answer] According to Excerpt 1, the requirements for graduation are as follows:

On recommendations of the FBS of the School, the University will award Master degree to the students who satisfy the following conditions:

1. Award of Degree. On recommendations of the FBS of the School, the University will award Master degree to the students who satisfy the following conditions:

a. Has been recommended by the respective institution.

b. On re-admission, the student will pay the prescribed admission fee and 50% tuition fee for the period of absence. The rate of the fee will be as applicable to students of the semester which s/he joins.

c. While considering the case for re-admission, it will be ascertained by the respective institution that the student has sufficient time to complete his/her degree within the prescribed time limit, which will start from the date of original/first admission.

d. No special classes/courses will be planned / arranged for such cases, other than planned classes for repeat courses.

e. After re-admission, his/her studies will be governed by the rules and regulations applicable to the entry s/he joins.

f. Suspension of registration, on grounds of prolonged absence, will be granted only once in the programme in which s/he was originally granted admission.

Therefore, the requirements for graduation are that the student must be recommended by the respective institution and meet the conditions for re-admission, if applicable.
