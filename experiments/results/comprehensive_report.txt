[Query 8] What are the requirements for graduation?
  [LLM Answer] According to Excerpt 1, the requirements for graduation are as follows:

On recommendations of the FBS of the School, the University will award Master degree to the students who satisfy the following conditions:

- For 16 years education graduates with Economics and Business Administration background: 36 credit hours, 1-4 years duration.
- For 16 years education graduates with a Psychology background: 38 credit hours, 1-4 years duration.
- For 16 years education graduates with Mass Communication/Journalism/Media Studies/ Public Relations background: 30 credit hours, 1-4 years duration.
- For 16 years education graduates with Master or Bachelors (Four Years) degree from any academic background: 30 credit hours, 2-4 years duration.
- For 16 years education graduates in Engineering/ BBA /MBA/B.Com from HEC/ PEC affiliated institution: 36 credit hours, 1-4 years duration.
- For 16 years education graduates with Master or Bachelor degree in relevant discipline: 36 credit hours, 2-4 years duration.
- For 16 years education graduates (Engineering, IT, Management and Social Sciences): 36 credit hours, 1-4 years duration.
- For 16 years education graduates with B. Arch degree from HEC recognized institute and accredited by PCATP: 43 credit hours, 1-4 years duration.

These requirements are specified in Excerpt 1, Chapter 4, Award of Master Degree in Business Administration/Executive Master in Business Administration/Social Sciences.
